About Espírito Santo

State of Espírito Santo

Espírito Santo is one of the 27 Brazilian states and is located in the Southeast Region of the country. It is the fourth smallest in terms of territorial extension, disregarding the Federal District. It has a coastline of 400 km , and the maritime environment has a great influence on its climate, which is predominantly humid tropical. With regard to vegetation cover, it has characteristics of the Atlantic Forest biome, in which the state is located. The economy of Espírito Santo is led by the industrial sector , with emphasis on the extractive industry. The state is also a major coffee producer.
